Romance is a Bonus Book ★★★★★

Synopsis

After losing her popularity as a copywriter, Kang Dani struggles to find a job, as she is overqualified with her past accomplishments. She finally lands a job at a publishing company after lying about her background. Cha Eunho, a genius writer, is the youngest chief editor at this publishing company and has known Dani since childhood. As the pair work together, they develop romantic feelings for each other.
